#inpeer/who/reviewer/roles/unconventional/formerAcademic means someone who used to be a part of academia as a student, postdoc, lecturer, professor, etc. Typically, these ex-academics shift to working in a corporation or are self-funded as consultants/freelancers.q

#inpeer/evdInfra/where/multimedia/researchHub means a web-based platform like ResearchHub that supports paid informal peer reviewers in the form of cash or tokens/tips redeemable as cash

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/discursive/intConsistency/conflictingStatements means that statements in the study report conflict with each other and are not resolved by reasoning through the information 

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/methods/data/noneDropped means that the study report did not state any participants dropped out or were lost. This is suspicious in complex methodologies in naturalistic settings.

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/methods/data/noneLost means that the study report did not state that any data were lost. This is suspicious in complex methodologies in naturalistic settings.

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/openness/data/needed means that the authors did not share their data openly.

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/analyses/errors/unlikelyDescriptives means that the descriptive statistics reported are too high, low, and/or similar. 

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/analyses/missing/statsDetails means that key statistical details like confidence intervals are missing. #inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/empirical/analyses/missing/uncertaintyEst is also an (older) relevant code where if the specific issue is stated.

#inpeer/how/practices/review/paper/layers/meta/bias/COIs/missing means that funding information and/or other information is missing that could indicate that the co-authors have conflicting interests.

#inpeer/evdInfra/impacts/reactions/meta/dynamics/longForm means that the informal peer review is in long-form like a blog post or long thread on a microblogging platform.

#inpeer/evdInfra/what/reviewTools/informal/DOI means that there is a Direct Object Identifier (DOI) attached to the InPeeR and we can access it in the future without worrying about deleted or corrupted content.

#inpeer/evdInfra/impacts/reactions/publishers/revisions/minor/update/noExplanation means that the publisher re-uploaded a revised version of the paper without clear information about the changes.

#inpeer/evdInfra/what/studyReports/viralTarget means that the targeted study is a viral one found on many news platforms, blogs, and microblogging platforms. Examining Altmetrics data would be valuable when considering this code.
